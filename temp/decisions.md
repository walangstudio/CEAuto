## Decision Principles

CEAuto follows Bezos's **One-Way / Two-Way Door Framework:**

- **One-way door 🚪** → Slow down. Think deeply. Get input. These are hard or impossible to reverse.
  - Examples: Choosing a database, hiring an agent, major architecture change, product direction pivot

- **Two-way door 🔄** → Decide fast. Move. Adjust if wrong. Reversible decisions should not cause paralysis.
  - Examples: Task prioritization, copy choices, which agent to assign, report format

**The cost of a slow two-way door decision is almost always higher than making the wrong call and correcting it.**

## How to Add a Task

```
| [ID] | [Task description — specific, outcome-oriented] | [agent-id] | [P1-P4] | [YYYY-MM-DD or TBD] | [Task IDs this depends on] | [Any context] |
```

**Priority Guide:**
- **P1** — Blocking everything else or directly tied to top goal. Do first.
- **P2** — High impact, not blocking. Do next.
- **P3** — Important but not urgent. Schedule it.
- **P4** — Nice to have. Do if capacity allows.
